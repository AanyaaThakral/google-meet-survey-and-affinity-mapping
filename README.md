Google Meet Pre-Join Experience

This project focuses on improving the Google Meet joining experience through a user-centered UX/UI design approach. Through user research, affinity mapping, and persona creation, several recurring pain points were identified, including audio issues, camera problems, internet instability, screen-sharing failures, and confusing settings before joining meetings.

To address these challenges, a Smart Pre-Join Check feature was designed that allows users to verify their microphone, camera, internet connection, and screen-sharing capabilities before entering a meeting. The solution aims to reduce technical disruptions, improve user confidence, and ensure a smoother meeting experience.

The project follows the complete UX design process, including research, analysis, persona development, wireframing, and prototyping, resulting in an intuitive and user-friendly pre-join workflow for Google Meet users.
